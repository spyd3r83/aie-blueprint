## The Throughline

Across both years and 110+ talks, the signal cuts through the noise:

- **The model is settled.** Stop optimizing which model you use. Start optimizing everything around it.
- **Context is the moat.** Not how you prompt — what information the model has and when.
- **Evals are the gate.** Without continuous, edge-case-rich evaluation, you have a demo.
- **The harness is the product.** Your scaffolding, guardrails, and feedback loops ARE the application.
- **Organizations must change.** Pilots don't become products without structural redesign.
